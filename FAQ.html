Got a question? We have an answer! Do not see your answer here, please feel free to visit the Contact page.

General:


Q: Where does the name Aquus come from?
A: The name AQQUS (Pronounced: Aqu-us) is a Portmanteau, the mixing of two words together, between the Latin words for horse, Equus, and the Latin word for water, Aqua. This word was inspired by ancient usage of horse as a mean for quick and efficient transportation; similarly, our case is design with the same purpose but for modern bathroom items. We hope that much like how horses were essential for early humans; our case will become a convenient part of your everyday commute.

Q: Why BiFold?
A: The BiFold got its name from how the case is design to act as a stand for your toiletry items but at the same act as a two-piece case that can be folded for easy portability and gives you convenient excess to your items.

Q: Where can I find support and warranty information for my case?
A: Support for the BiFold can be found in User Manual under the support and warranty sections. For generals question please visit the Contact Us section of the website to contact our team.

Q: Is there some way to view the product before purchasing?
